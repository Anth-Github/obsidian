- Press `Ctrl + Shift + I` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd + Opt + I` (Mac)
    
- Click on the **Network** tab
    

Or, right-click on the webpage and select **Inspect** or **Inspect Element**, then navigate to the Network tab.
